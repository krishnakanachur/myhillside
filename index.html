How we prepare

A story of patience, warmth, and proportion.

Each batch is made to feel considered. We keep the process honest so the final cup feels layered, rounded, and unmistakably South Indian.

01

Handpicked coffee seeds

We begin with carefully selected beans that carry the strength and aroma expected from Malenadu-grown coffee.

02

Sun-dried for days

The beans are dried gradually to preserve their natural oils, helping build body and fragrance without rushing the process.

03

Slowly roasted

Roasting is kept slow and controlled so the blend develops a fuller profile with depth, balance, and warmth.

04

Finished with 30% chicory

Our final 70:30 ratio creates the classic thick decoction, strong aroma, and smooth lingering finish that defines a premium filter coffee experience.
